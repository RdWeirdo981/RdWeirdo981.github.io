How to Use This Website

Posted on 2021-01-10 Edited on 2023-01-06 In 5. General

Welcome to my personal study library!

I want to use this website to record my study notes of cryptography/mathematics/infosec. There are two main approaches to quickly go through the contents: Categories and Tags.

Categories are sorted by the way that I learn these stuffs, for example, course code will be a factor of it. The current categories are as following:

  1. Study Notes5
  2. Research Notes2
    • Paper I Read1
  3. Catch The Flag4
    • CTF-Crypto4
  4. Master of Cybersecurity12
    • FIT5003 Software Security4
    • FIT5124 Advanced Topics in Security2
    • ...
  5. General2
  6. Resources3
  7. Virtual Internship1

I will update these categories as I writing more articles. It is usual that some articles may have various categories.

Tags are sorted by the feature or topic that the article focus on. For instance, I will tag my report of assignment 2 of 5003 as #SQLi# #XSS# #CSRF#.

This blog also contains About page which simply describes who I am and what I do.

If you want to go through all the articles by chronological order, you can use Archives page.

UPDATE:

I've also collected many resources during my master thesis, thus there's also a page Resources containing all the materials that I collected to learn different perspectives of cryptography. I also use this webpage to explain my learning progress, so it is a bit messy there. Apologies.
